# PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide
- Account information: name, email, company
- Payment information: billing address (payment details processed by secure third parties)
- Communications: support requests, feedback
- Usage data: logs of your use of Services

1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- Device information: IP address, browser type, operating system
- Usage analytics: pages visited, time spent, features used
- Cookies and similar technologies

## 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use information for:
- Providing and improving Services
- Billing and payment processing
- Customer support
- Security and fraud prevention
- Legal compliance
- Marketing (with your consent)

## 3. DATA RETENTION

3.1 Retention Periods
- Account data: retained during active account and 30 days after termination
- Transactional data: retained for 7 years for tax and legal purposes
- Usage logs: retained for 12 months
- Deleted data: removed from active systems within 48 hours, backups within 90 days

3.2 Your Right to Delete
You may request deletion of your personal data except where:
- Required by law
- Necessary for service provision
- Needed for business operations

## 4. DATA SHARING

4.1 We Do Not Sell Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information.

4.2 Third Parties
We may share information with:
- Service providers (hosting, payment processing, support)
- Legal authorities (required by law)
- Business partners (with your consent)

4.3 Data Processing Agreements
All service providers are bound by Data Processing Agreements requiring confidentiality and appropriate security.

## 5. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

5.1 Access
You have the right to access your personal data.

5.2 Correction
You can request correction of inaccurate information.

5.3 Deletion
You can request deletion, subject to legal retention requirements.

5.4 Portability
You have the right to receive your data in a structured, portable format.

5.5 Opt-Out
You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

## 6. GDPR COMPLIANCE

For residents of the EU, we comply with GDPR including:
- Lawful basis for processing
- Data Protection Impact Assessments
- Data Protection Officer contact
- Rights regarding profiling and automated decision-making

## 7. CCPA COMPLIANCE

For California residents, we comply with CCPA including:
- Disclosure of information practices
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale or sharing

## 8. COOKIES AND TRACKING

8.1 Cookie Usage
We use cookies for:
- Session management
- User preferences
- Analytics
- Marketing

8.2 Cookie Control
You can disable cookies in your browser, though this may affect functionality.

8.3 Do Not Track
We honor Do Not Track signals in your browser.

## 9. SECURITY

We implement:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Access controls
- Regular security assessments
- Employee training
- Incident response procedures

However, no security is 100% guaranteed.

## 10. CONTACT US

For privacy questions or requests:
- Email: privacy@company.com
- Response time: 30 days
