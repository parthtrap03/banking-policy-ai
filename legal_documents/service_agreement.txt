# MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Parties: Your Company Inc. and Customer
Last Updated: 2024-01-15

## 1. TERMS OF SERVICE

1.1 Service Provision
The Company agrees to provide cloud-based software services ("Services") as described in the applicable Statement of Work (SOW). Services include hosting, maintenance, support, and updates as outlined in Appendix A.

1.2 Service Availability
The Company guarantees 99.5% uptime measured monthly, excluding scheduled maintenance and events beyond reasonable control. Emergency maintenance may occur with 4 hours notice.

1.3 User Responsibilities
The Customer is responsible for:
- Maintaining accurate account information
- Protecting login credentials
- Ensuring compliance with applicable laws
- Proper use of Services in accordance with this Agreement

## 2. PAYMENT TERMS

2.1 Fees
Fees are based on the pricing schedule in the SOW and are billed monthly in advance on the first day of each month.

2.2 Billing and Payment
- Invoices are due within 30 days of invoice date
- Payments should be made via ACH, wire transfer, or credit card
- Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the maximum rate allowed by law

2.3 Taxes
All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes. Customer is responsible for any sales, VAT, or other taxes applicable to the Services.

## 3. CONFIDENTIALITY

3.1 Definition
"Confidential Information" means non-public information disclosed by one party that is marked as confidential or reasonably understood to be confidential.

3.2 Obligations
Each party agrees to:
- Maintain confidentiality of Confidential Information
- Use it only for purposes of this Agreement
- Limit access to employees with a need to know
- Protect it with reasonable security measures

3.3 Exceptions
Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that is:
- Publicly available
- Independently developed
- Received from third parties without confidentiality obligations
- Required to be disclosed by law

## 4. LIABILITY LIMITATIONS

4.1 Disclaimer of Warranties
EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY PROVIDED, THE COMPANY MAKES NO WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR PARTICULAR PURPOSE, OR NON-INFRINGEMENT.

4.2 Limitation of Liability
EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY OR IP INFRINGEMENT, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR:
- Indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages
- Lost profits, data, or business opportunities
- Damages exceeding fees paid in prior 12 months
- Costs of substitute goods or services

4.3 Maximum Liability
Total liability for either party shall not exceed the fees paid by Customer in the 12 months preceding the claim.

## 5. TERMINATION

5.1 Term
This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for one year, automatically renewing for additional one-year terms unless either party provides 30 days written notice of non-renewal.

5.2 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately if:
- The other party materially breaches and fails to cure within 30 days of written notice
- The other party becomes insolvent or bankrupt

5.3 Termination for Convenience
Customer may terminate for convenience with 60 days written notice and payment of:
- All fees through the notice period
- 25% of remaining contract value, if any

5.4 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- Customer must stop using Services
- Company will return or delete Confidential Information
- Provisions 3, 4, and 6 survive termination

## 6. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

6.1 Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without regard to conflict of law principles.

6.2 Jurisdiction
Both parties consent to exclusive jurisdiction in the state and federal courts located in San Francisco County, California.

6.3 Arbitration
Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration administered by JAMS in San Francisco under its Streamlined Arbitration Rules. The arbitrator's decision is final and binding.

6.4 Attorney Fees
The prevailing party in any legal action shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney fees and costs.

## 7. DATA PROTECTION

7.1 Data Processing
Company processes Customer Data in accordance with the Data Processing Addendum (DPA) attached as Appendix B.

7.2 Security
Company implements industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and logging
- Incident response procedures

7.3 Data Breach Notification
Company will notify Customer of data breaches within 48 hours of discovery.
